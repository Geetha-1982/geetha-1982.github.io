About Us

Unlocking Full Mental Potential

At S&S Mind Matics, we offer dynamic and engaging Abacus and Vedic Maths programs specially designed for young learners to unlock their full mental potential.

Our classes are thoughtfully scheduled twice a week for 45 minutes each — the perfect balance of consistency and convenience. We provide both online and offline learning modes, letting students choose what suits them best.

Each course is led by our certified Head Instructor, Geetha. R. Rudgi, who uses child-friendly methods that make learning enjoyable, interactive, and result-driven — whether your child chooses Abacus, Vedic Maths, or both.
